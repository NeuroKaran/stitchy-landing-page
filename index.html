Why we built Stitchy 🇮🇳

Tracking Indian food on traditional apps like MyFitnessPal is incredibly frustrating.

Western trackers fail to account for hidden ghee, cooking oils, and the vast complexity of an Indian Thali. You end up searching for "1 katori dal" and picking a random option that is wildly inaccurate.

We wanted to create a tracker that felt incredible to use—as easy as taking a photo and leaving a quick voice note about what you ate. The AI does all the heavy lifting.

THE CORE FEATURE

AI-Powered Health Insights 🧠

Stitchy doesn't just count calories. It uses your food data to predict how your meals impact your health, mood, and energy — turning every logged plate into a data point for smarter living.

Health Score Predictor

Every day, Stitchy calculates a dynamic Health Score (0–100) based on your meal composition, micronutrient balance, and dietary consistency.

  • Nutrient density analysis — Are you getting enough iron, fiber, vitamins from your dal, sabzi, and greens?
  • Pattern recognition — Tracks how your score trends over 7-day and 30-day rolling windows
  • Explainable AI — Every score comes with a plain-English summary: "Score dropped because of low protein and excess refined carbs yesterday"

Mood & Energy Forecasting

The AI correlates your food choices with self-reported mood and energy check-ins, discovering patterns you can't see yourself.

  • Post-meal energy tracking — Log how you feel after meals; AI finds the foods that drain or boost you
  • Sugar crash detection — Identifies meals that cause afternoon energy crashes from refined carb spikes
  • Weekly mood reports — Visual charts showing the correlation between your diet and emotional well-being

FAQ

Is Stitchy only for weight loss?

No. Stitchy supports lose, maintain, and gain goals with profile-based calorie and macro targets.

Can I log meals without speaking?

Yes. You can scan with image only, though voice context can improve estimate quality.

Does Stitchy support Indian cuisine well?

Yes. The nutrition analysis flow is optimized for Indian dishes, portions, and cooking context.

How accurate are the AI health predictions?

The predictions improve over time as you log more meals. With 7+ days of data, the health score and mood patterns become highly reliable. The AI always explains its reasoning so you stay in control.

When will wearable integration be available?

We're actively building Apple Watch and Google Fit integrations. Early access will roll out to Pro subscribers first. Join the waitlist to be notified.
