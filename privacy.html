Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters deeply to us. Here's exactly how we handle your data.

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Stitchy ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA) of India.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Info: Name, email, age, gender, dietary preferences.
  • Health Data: Weight, height, activity level, calorie/macro targets, food logs, health scores, mood/energy check-ins.
  • Meal Data: Photos and voice recordings for AI analysis.
  • Usage Data: App interactions and anonymised analytics.
  • Device Data: Device type, OS, app version.
  • Wearable Data (future): Sleep, heart rate, activity — only when you explicitly enable sync.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • AI-powered nutritional analysis of your meals
  • Personalised health scores, mood forecasts, and dietary recommendations
  • TDEE and macro target calculations
  • Historical trends and progress charts
  • AI model improvement (only with explicit consent)
  • Service updates and support

4. Consent & Control

Per DPDPA Section 6, consent is free, specific, informed, and unambiguous. You control your data via our Consent Manager — opt in/out of AI training data retention, review permissions anytime, no pre-ticked boxes.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Transient processing: Raw photos and audio are deleted immediately after AI analysis unless you consent to retention.
  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 in transit, transparent encryption at rest.
  • Secure proxy: No API keys exposed client-side.
  • Access control: Strictly limited to authorised personnel.

6. Cross-Border Transfers

AI inference may use Google servers outside India. Anonymised data only. We ensure DPDPA-compliant transfer mechanisms and adequate protection standards.

7. Retention & Deletion

  • Account data retained while active. Raw media deleted immediately after analysis.
  • Account deletion: all data permanently expunged within 30 days, including backups.

8. Your Rights

Under the DPDPA 2023: access, correction, erasure, consent withdrawal, and grievance redressal. Contact: privacy@stitchy.app

9. Children's Privacy

Not intended for users under 18. Data from minors is deleted immediately upon discovery.

10. Changes & Contact

Material changes communicated via in-app notification. Contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@stitchy.app.
