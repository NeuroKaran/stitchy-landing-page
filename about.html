ABOUT US

The story behind Stitchy

We're building the smartest calorie tracker for the 1.4 billion people who eat Indian food every day.

Why Indian food tracking is broken 🍛

Every calorie tracking app on the market was built for Western diets. They work great if you eat a sandwich or a bowl of oatmeal. But try logging a thali with 6 different dishes, hidden ghee, and home-ground masalas — and they fall apart completely.

We experienced this frustration firsthand. Searching for "1 katori dal" on MyFitnessPal returns dozens of wildly different entries. HealthifyMe locks basic tracking behind expensive paywalls. Cal AI can't tell the difference between palak paneer and saag.

Stitchy was born from this pain. We asked a simple question: what if tracking a meal was as easy as taking a photo and leaving a quick voice note?

Our approach: Multimodal AI 🧠

Instead of relying on images alone (which fail for complex Indian meals) or manual search (which is painfully slow), Stitchy fuses two modalities for unprecedented accuracy.

📸

Visual Recognition

Computer vision identifies the dishes on your plate, detecting rotis, rice, curries, and sides automatically.

🎤

Voice Context

Your voice note adds the context cameras can't see — "extra ghee", "half portion", "no cream in the dal".

⚡

AI Fusion

Gemini 2.5 Flash processes both inputs simultaneously, cross-referencing visual data with spoken modifiers for accurate results in under 5 seconds.

Beyond calories: AI Health Insights 🔮

Tracking calories is just the beginning. Stitchy's AI analyses your food data over time to predict how your diet affects your health, mood, and energy levels.

Every meal you log becomes a data point. The AI identifies patterns — like how skipping protein at lunch causes afternoon energy crashes, or how high-sugar dinners correlate with poor sleep quality.

And we're just getting started. Our roadmap includes wearable integrations — pulling sleep data, calories burned, and heart rate from your smartwatch to create a truly holistic health prediction engine.

What we believe

The principles that guide every feature we build.

🎯 Accuracy over speed

We'd rather take an extra second to get the macros right than give you a fast, wrong answer. The multimodal approach exists because precision matters.

🔒 Privacy by design

Your food photos and voice recordings are processed transiently and deleted immediately after analysis. We comply fully with the DPDPA 2023. Your health data is yours.

💚 Fair pricing

We'll never lock basic calorie tracking behind a paywall. Free users get real AI-powered tracking, not a crippled demo. Pro exists for power users who want deeper insights.
