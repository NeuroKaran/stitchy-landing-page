A note on pricing 💬

We believe great health tracking shouldn't be locked behind expensive paywalls. That's why our Free plan includes full photo + voice AI logging — not a crippled demo.

Pro exists for power users who want deeper AI insights, mood tracking, and unlimited everything. We use the revenue to keep improving our AI models and expanding the Indian food database.

Pricing FAQ

Can I switch between plans?

Yes. You can upgrade to Pro at any time and your data carries over seamlessly. You can also downgrade back to Free if needed.

Is there a yearly plan?

We're working on an annual plan with a significant discount. Stay tuned — it's coming soon.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular wallets via our secure payment partner.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 7 days of your subscription for a full refund, no questions asked.

Will the price change?

Early subscribers lock in their price. If we ever raise prices, existing Pro members keep their original rate.
